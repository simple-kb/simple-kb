When one discusses/deliberates with someone, one implicitly recognizes their individual sovereignty.

In its weak form, this axiom becomes:
"Accepting the conditions of an argumentative debate demonstrates a preference for peaceful resolution of conflicts rather than power struggles or violence."
https://www.wikiberal.org/wiki/%C3%89thique_de_l'argumentation

This interpretation is sufficient to justify an ethics of non-aggression ([[Being and ought-to-be]])
