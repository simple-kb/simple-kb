Growth of what? Of annual GDP.
- in a free society there is no GDP.
Who needs to grow France's annual GDP today?
The State. The State goes into debt every year (in our name). To reassure investors, it needs to present figures.
The State increases its spending every year, and must therefore increase its revenue every year. Through VAT for a large part...
- in a free society, there is no public debt.

Many civil servants (national education for example) denounce the ideology of growth, and demand sobriety (notably through environmentalism). While their salaries, their pensions depend directly on the famous growth.
