And we see that apparent propositions like: “*a* = *a*”, “*a* = *b* . *b* = *c* . ⊃ *a* = *c*”, “(*x*) . *x* = *x*”, “(∃*x*) . *x* = *a*”, etc. cannot be written in a correct logical notation at all.
