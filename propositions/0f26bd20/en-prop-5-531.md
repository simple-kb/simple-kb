I write therefore not “*f*(*a*, *b*) . *a* = *b*” but “*f*(*a*, *a*)” (or “*f*(*b*, *b*)”). And not “*f*(*a*, *b*) . \~*a* = *b*” but “*f*(*a*, *b*)”.
