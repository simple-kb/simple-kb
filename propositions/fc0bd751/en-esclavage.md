**Slavery** or the slavery phenomenon refers to the condition or status of persons, called slaves, who are both legally without personality and deprived of [rights](https://www.wikiberal.org/wiki/Droit "Rights"), or treated as a form of [property](https://www.wikiberal.org/wiki/Propri%C3%A9t%C3%A9 "Property") of another person, designated master
