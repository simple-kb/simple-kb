Instead of “(*x*) : *fx* ⊃ *x* = *a*” we therefore write *e.g.* “(∃*x*) . *fx* . ⊃ . *fa* : \~(∃*x*, *y*) . *fx* . *fy*”.

And the proposition “*only* one *x* satisfies *f*( )” reads: “(∃*x*) . *fx* : \~(∃*x*, *y*) . *fx* . *fy*”.
