An individual must exercise their consent towards any action that affects their [[Individual Sovereignty]].

The non-aggression principle is the principle of non-initiation of violence. https://www.wikiberal.org/wiki/Principe_de_non-agression

Therefore an individual must exercise their consent regarding any action that affects or constrains:
* Their life and physical integrity
* Their decisions and actions
* Their faculties and their work
