With regard to the existence of *n* atomic facts there are ![{ K_n = \sum_{\nu=0}^n \binom{n}{\nu} }](images/109747f0cdbcfd62f1bce67d93eb51350f5f3835c6459539f96d2dc70d84420f.svg)\  possibilities.

It is possible for all combinations of atomic facts to exist, and the others not to exist.
