The names are the simple symbols, I indicate them by single letters (*x*, *y*, *z*).

The elementary proposition I write as function of the names, in the form “*fx*”, “*ϕ*(*x*, *y*)”, etc.

Or I indicate it by the letters *p*, *q*, *r*.
